The Story

Where Coffee Meets Skin Alchemy.

Born from a kitchen experiment, perfected into a luxury ritual.

Noir Elixir began with a simple question: what if your skin could feel the same awakening your first espresso gives you?


Each bottle of Espresso Radiance Oil starts with carefully selected coffee, gently roasted and steeped in slow-infused coconut oil. No shortcuts, no harsh heat—just time, patience, and the quiet magic of natural extraction.


The result is an oil that doesn’t just sit on the surface. It melts in, softens, and brightens—leaving behind a velvety glow and that subtle, addictive coffee warmth.

Ingredient Philosophy

We keep our ingredient list intentionally short, so every drop has a purpose:

The Ritual

How to Use Espresso Radiance Oil.

A 30-second ritual that feels like a café for your skin.

  1. 1

    Prep on a clean canvas.

    Start with freshly cleansed, slightly damp skin. Pat gently with a towel—no harsh rubbing.

  2. 2

    Warm the brew.

    Take 2–3 drops in your palms. Rub together to gently warm the oil and awaken the aroma.

  3. 3

    Press, don’t drag.

    Press into your face and neck, then smooth outwards in upward motions. Let it sink in.

  4. 4

    Glow and go.

    Use alone at night, or under sunscreen and makeup during the day for a soft, lit-from-within radiance.

Always patch test on a small area before first use. Not recommended for very acne-prone or sensitised skin without consulting a skin professional.

When to use

✦ As a nightly facial oil for a deep, comforting glow.
✦ Mixed with your moisturiser for a softer finish.
✦ As a pre-makeup radiance layer on special evenings.
